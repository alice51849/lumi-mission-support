Why families love Lumi Mission Planet

FAQ

Frequently asked questions 常見問題

Everything about gameplay, the custom task, purchase, languages and privacy.

How does the app work, and for what ages?這個 App 怎麼玩?適合幾歲?+
Designed for ages 3–7. Pick a routine — Eat a Meal, Brush Teeth, Sleep Time, Clean Up or School Ready — and your child taps to feed Lumi each time they finish in real life. Lumi grows through five stages, builds daily streaks, and earns collectible stickers. A parent dashboard shows a 7-day calendar and a full completion log. 專為 3–7 歲設計。選一個日常(吃飯、刷牙、睡覺、收玩具、上學準備),孩子每在現實中完成一次就點一下餵養 Lumi。Lumi 會經歷五個成長階段、累積連續天數、收集貼紙;家長後台有 7 日日曆與完整完成紀錄。
Can I add my own custom task?可以自己新增任務嗎?+
Yes. Open the Parent Dashboard and type your family's own Custom Task — bath time, piano practice, saying thank you — anything your routine needs. It appears right alongside the built-in missions. 可以。進入家長後台輸入你家專屬的「自訂任務」——洗澡、練琴、說謝謝,任何日常都行,會和內建任務並列顯示。
What languages are supported?支援哪些語言?+
English, 繁體中文, 日本語 and 한국어. The app automatically detects your device's language on first launch, and you can switch any time in the Parent Dashboard. 英文、繁體中文、日文、韓文。App 首次啟動會自動偵測系統語言,也可在家長後台隨時手動切換。
Is it a one-time purchase? Any subscription or ads?是一次買斷嗎?有訂閱或廣告嗎?+
The first mission is free. Unlock all five missions, custom tasks and the full sticker book with a single one-time purchase — there are no subscriptions and no ads of any kind. 第一個任務免費。用一次性買斷即可解鎖全部五項任務、自訂任務與完整貼紙圖鑑——沒有訂閱、沒有任何廣告。
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?換新裝置如何恢復購買?+
On the unlock screen, tap "Restore Purchase" while signed in with the same Apple ID — Apple won't charge again for something you already own. Note that on-device progress does not transfer automatically between devices. 在解鎖頁點「恢復購買」,並用原本的 Apple ID 登入即可,已購買的內容不會再次收費。請注意裝置上的進度不會自動跨裝置轉移。
Does it collect any personal data?會蒐集個人資料嗎?+
No. The app collects no personal data — no accounts, no ads, no third-party tracking. All progress is stored only on your device. Photos and notifications are used only if you turn them on yourself (see the privacy policy below). 不會。App 不蒐集任何個人資料,無帳號、無廣告、無第三方追蹤,所有進度只存在你的裝置上。相簿與通知僅在你自己開啟時才使用(詳見下方隱私政策)。

01 What we store, and where

The app stores your child's progress — completed tasks, streaks, growth stage, collected stickers, your custom task, settings, and the local timestamps of each completion — only on your device, using on-device local storage. This information is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and it is removed when you delete the app. An optional "Export CSV" button in the parent area lets you save a copy of the completion log as a plain text file; that file is created locally and goes only where you choose to send it. We never receive it. App 僅在裝置本機(on-device local storage)儲存孩子的進度(已完成任務、連續天數、成長階段、收集的貼紙、你的自訂任務、設定,以及每次完成的本地時間)。此資料不會傳送給我們或任何第三方,並在你刪除 App 時一併移除。家長區的「匯出 CSV」按鈕可把完成紀錄存成純文字檔,該檔案在本機產生、只會送到你自己選擇的地方,我們不會收到。

02 No data collection, no ads, no tracking

Lumi Mission Planet does not collect, use, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no advertising, no behavioral profiling, no analytics, no attribution, and no third-party tracking of any kind. Our App Privacy label on the App Store is therefore "Data Not Collected." 「Lumi 任務星球」不蒐集、不使用、不傳輸、不販售、不分享任何個人資料。沒有帳號、登入、廣告、行為分析、數據分析、歸因,也沒有任何第三方追蹤。因此 App Store 上的隱私標籤為「未蒐集資料(Data Not Collected)」。

03 Device permissions

Photos (optional) — only used if you tap "Save Card" to save a victory picture to your own photo library. The app only adds an image you asked it to save; it never reads, browses, or uploads your photos. Notifications (optional) — only used for the gentle daily reminder you turn on yourself in the Parent Dashboard. Reminders are scheduled locally on the device; no data leaves the phone. Both permissions are entirely optional and the app works fully without them. 相簿(選用)——僅在你點「儲存卡片」把勝利圖存到自己的相簿時使用;App 只會新增你要求儲存的圖片,絕不讀取、瀏覽或上傳你的照片。通知(選用)——僅用於你自己在家長後台開啟的每日提醒,提醒在裝置本機排程,沒有任何資料離開手機。兩種權限皆為選用,不開啟也能完整使用 App。

04 Third-party software we use

The app is built with the open-source Expo and React Native frameworks (including their standard on-device modules for audio, haptics, local storage, blur effects, image capture, language detection, and local notifications). It contains no advertising, analytics, attribution, social-media, or crash-reporting SDKs — for example, no Google AdMob, no Firebase Analytics, no Facebook SDK, no Sentry. These components run locally to display the app; they do not collect, transmit, or sell your child's personal data. 本 App 以開源的 Expo 與 React Native 框架開發(含其標準本機模組:音訊、觸覺回饋、本機儲存、毛玻璃效果、影像擷取、語言偵測、本機通知等)。不含任何廣告、分析、歸因、社群或當機回報 SDK——例如沒有 Google AdMob、沒有 Firebase Analytics、沒有 Facebook SDK、沒有 Sentry。這些元件僅在本機運作以顯示畫面,不會蒐集、傳輸或販售孩子的個人資料。

05 Purchases

Lumi Mission Planet offers a single one-time purchase to unlock all missions. Any transaction is handled entirely by the Apple App Store; we never receive or store your payment information, card number, or Apple ID. 「Lumi 任務星球」提供一次性買斷以解鎖全部任務。任何交易完全由 Apple App Store 處理,我們不會收到或儲存你的付款資訊、信用卡號或 Apple ID。

06 Children's privacy

The app is designed for young children (around ages 3 to 7). Because it collects no personal information at all and contains no third-party ads or tracking, it complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K. Actions that lead outside the app, and the one-time purchase, are placed behind a parental long-press gate. 本 App 為幼兒(約 3 到 7 歲)設計。因完全不蒐集個人資訊、且無任何第三方廣告或追蹤,符合美國 COPPA 與歐盟 GDPR-K。任何會離開 App 的動作與一次性購買,都設有家長長按關卡。
