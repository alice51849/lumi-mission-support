Welcome

About Lumi 關於 Lumi

Lumi Mission Planet turns everyday routines — eating, brushing teeth, sleeping, tidying up and getting ready for school — into a joyful game. Every time your child finishes a real-life task, they tap to feed and nurture Lumi the space bear, who grows happier and bigger over time. Streaks, collectible stickers and gentle celebrations keep kids motivated, while a parent dashboard keeps you in control.

Lumi 任務星球把吃飯、刷牙、睡覺、收玩具、上學準備這些日常,變成一場療癒的小遊戲。孩子每完成一件真實任務,就點一下餵養太空小熊 Lumi,陪牠一天天長大、變開心。連續天數、收集貼紙與溫柔的慶祝動畫讓孩子持續有動力,家長後台則讓你完全掌握。

How do I unlock all missions? 如何解鎖全部任務?
The first mission is free. Tap “Unlock all missions”, pass the parent gate (long-press) and complete the one-time purchase — no subscription, ever. 第一個任務免費。點「解鎖全部任務」,通過家長長按閘門,完成一次性買斷即可,永不訂閱。
Can I add my own task? 可以自己新增任務嗎?
Yes. Open the Parent dashboard and type your own custom task — perfect for routines unique to your family. 可以。進入家長後台輸入你的自訂任務,完美對應你家獨有的日常。
Will it work in my language? 支援我的語言嗎?
Lumi auto-detects your device language and supports English, 繁體中文, 日本語 and 한국어. You can switch any time in the Parent dashboard. Lumi 會自動偵測系統語言,支援英文、繁體中文、日文、韓文,也可在家長後台隨時手動切換。
How do I restore my purchase? 如何恢復購買?
Open the unlock screen and tap “Restore Purchase”, signed in with the same Apple ID. 在解鎖頁點「恢復購買」,並使用原本的 Apple ID 登入即可。

Privacy

Privacy Policy 隱私權政策

Last updated: 8 June 2026 · 最後更新:2026 年 6 月 8 日

Lumi Mission Planet is built for children and designed to be radically private. We do not collect, store on our servers, or share any personal information. The app has no accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party advertising.

Lumi 任務星球專為兒童設計,以「極致隱私」為原則。我們不蒐集、不上傳到伺服器、也不分享任何個人資料。App 沒有帳號、沒有分析 SDK、沒有第三方廣告。

Data stays on your device 資料只留在裝置上

  • All progress — completed tasks, streaks, stickers, your custom task and completion timestamps — is stored locally on the device only, never transmitted. 所有進度(已完成任務、連續天數、貼紙、自訂任務、完成時間紀錄)只存在本機裝置,絕不外傳。
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data. 移除 App 即永久刪除這些資料。

Device permissions 裝置權限

  • Photos (optional): only used if you tap “Save Card” to save a victory picture to your own photo library. Lumi never reads or uploads your photos. 相簿(選用):僅在你點「儲存卡片」把勝利圖存到自己的相簿時使用,Lumi 不會讀取或上傳你的照片。
  • Notifications (optional): only used for the local daily reminder you turn on yourself. Reminders are scheduled on-device; no data leaves the phone. 通知(選用):僅用於你自己開啟的每日提醒,提醒在裝置本機排程,沒有任何資料離開手機。

Children's privacy 兒童隱私

  • No personal data is requested from children. Purchases and external links sit behind a parental long-press gate. 不向兒童索取任何個人資料,購買與外部連結皆位於家長長按閘門之後。
  • Compliant in spirit with Apple's Kids Category guidelines, COPPA and GDPR-K — because the simplest way to protect data is to never collect it. 精神上符合 Apple 兒童類別、COPPA 與 GDPR-K — 因為保護資料最好的方式,就是從不蒐集。
